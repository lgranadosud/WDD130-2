Purpose

This website will serve the purpose of promoting a clothing brand that helps people to find a brand that fit their culture and lifestyle. Also, it will be benefitial because it will be a great way to promote the merch and an easier way to sell it too.

Audience

People of all ages, that are looking for clothes that match their lifestyle and cultures. They usually acces the pages via Laptop and mobile devices. Also this brand targets people from low to high finantintial situations that love rafting.
